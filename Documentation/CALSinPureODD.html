    1.1.5. <entry>

    <entry> (Entry in Table) Identifies an <entry> in a <row> of a <thead>, <tfoot>, or <tbody> in a <tgroup> or <entrytbl>. Default values for colsep, rowsep, valign, align, char, and charoff may come from like-named attributes from the nearest element identified as influencing this <entry>. The values may come from <table> (or a tabstyle therein), <tgroup> (or a tgroupstyle therein), <spanspec> (defining the spanname), <colspec/> (defining namest or colname), <tbody>, <row>, or when within an <entrytbl> those elements so contained, or its tgroupstyle. Note that <colspec/> and <spanspec> are not strictly in the element lineage.
